Rules to play

1.Guess the WORDLE in six tries

2.Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.Hit enter button to submit

3.After each guess,the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word

4.A correct letter turns green

5.A correct letter in the wrong place turns yellow

6.An incorrect letter turns gray

7.Letter can be used more than once
